    Step-by-step: How to delete a Google Business Profile

    Step 1: Remove managers and owners

    1. Open Google and type "my business" into the search box or open your business in the Google Business Profile Manager.
    2. Your business profile should now appear. If not, ensure you are logged into your Google account.
    3. On the top right corner click on the three dots.
    4. Click on "People and access".
    5. In this menu remove all managers and people.

    Step 2: Mark your business as closed and remove it

    1. Navigate to "Remove Business Profile" and then "Remove profile content and managers".
    2. Select "Mark your business as permanently closed", select "Continue" and confirm.

    Why can't I delete a Google Business Profile by myself?

    With the method described you will lose access to the GMB profile; however, the listing will still remain visible in Google Search and Google Maps. Google, and especially its navigation service Google Maps, relies on the data and businesses listed on the platform. There is no option to delete the profile yourself; it can only be marked as "Permanently closed". Marking a business as "Permanently closed" may not be the right approach in every case, as customers might get confused.

    Removing a business from Google Maps

    You may have asked yourself if the business listing on Google Search and on Google Maps is the same, and yes, you are true. Google Maps is backed with tons of data of businesses all around the world to help people to navigate through the streets. There may be some reasons why you want to remove your business from Google Maps, e.g. your profile does contain wrong information, you have reviews you want to get removed or you just don't need the profile. If you want to remove a business from Google Maps, just follow the steps to remove a business profile in general and suggest an edit to the profile afterwards.

    How to suggest an edit on Google Maps:

    1. Search for your business in Google Maps.
    2. Scroll down and click on "Suggest an edit".
    3. Select "Close or remove".
    4. Select "Doesn't exist here".

    Common issues with the verification of your Google Business Profile

    Google My Business profiles need to be verified first to change any details and to get access to the profile. In some cases, Google needs reverification from time to time. While the verification process is easy to handle most of the time, there can be some challenges.

    The different verification methods:

    • Phone number: You will receive a call or SMS from Google (automated voice) and have to enter a code on your screen (6 digit).
    • Email: The email address must be part of the website shown on your Google Business Profile, e.g. info@yourdomain.com (5 digit).
    • Business video: Google asks you to make a video of your business including the street number and signage (takes about 12 days to be verified by Google).
    • Postcard: this method is not available anymore and was a common verification method in the past few years.

    I'm getting the message "Google is processing your verification. It usually takes a few minutes.":

    • After completing the verification process Google will check the details.
    • This can take up to 5 days.
    • If it takes longer, consider contacting Google directly under this link: Google Business Profile Support. Usually, you will get an answer via email within 2 days.
